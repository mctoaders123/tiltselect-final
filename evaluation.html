TiltSelect Evaluation

Welcome

This is a short study for a Mobile HCI coursework project at the University of Glasgow.

What you will do: tilt your phone to move through a menu, then try two ways of selecting an item: shaking the phone and making a sound. It should take about 5 to 10 minutes.

What is recorded: task times, accuracy, skipped trials, and answers to a short questionnaire. No personal details are collected.

Taking part is optional: you can stop at any point by closing the page. Nothing is submitted until the end.
